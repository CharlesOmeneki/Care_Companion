Top FAQs

How do I edit my profile?

Sign in, go to your profile (top-right) and choose Edit profile. Update fields and save; changes appear immediately.

How do I cancel a booking?

Open the booking, click Cancel and follow the prompts. Cancellation fees may apply depending on provider policy.

I forgot my password — what now?

Use the 'Forgot password' link on the sign-in page to receive a reset email. If you don't receive it, check your spam or contact support using the button on the right.

How do I report a concern about a caregiver?

If it's urgent, call our safety line at +2349132272022. Otherwise file a report via your dashboard or email support with reference numbers and details.
