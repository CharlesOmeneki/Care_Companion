Frequently asked

Do you provide background checks?
Yes. We perform background and reference checks for every caregiver and family we recommend. Verification is completed before we share personal details or connect either party.
How do payments work?
Payment is agreed between family and caregiver. We recommend standard hourly rates and can advise invoicing options. We do not handle payments on behalf of users unless explicitly stated.
Do you operate internationally?
Yes — we support multiple countries. Local regulations apply; we provide onboarding guidance for your region and ensure candidates meet local requirements when possible.
Is this service online-only?
Our platform is online-first: we match, vet and introduce caregivers and families using our digital tools. All initial matching, interviews and paperwork are handled online. If local in-person support is required, we advise on next steps but do not provide in-person services directly.
When will I get matched?
After you submit a request we typically shortlist candidates within 24–72 hours depending on availability and location. We’ll reach out with verified matches and suggested next steps.
